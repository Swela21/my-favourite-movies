Thulani's Favourite Movies

Zack Snyder's Justice League

Zack Snyder's Justice League follows Batman and Wonder Woman as they gather a team—Aquaman, Cyborg, and The Flash—to stop the alien warlord Steppenwolf from uniting three powerful Mother Boxes and destroying Earth. The heroes resurrect Superman, and together they defeat Steppenwolf while facing the greater looming threat of Darkseid.

Cars 3

Cars 3 follows Lightning McQueen as he struggles to keep up with a new generation of faster racers, especially the talented Jackson Storm. After a major crash, McQueen trains with a young, enthusiastic coach named Cruz Ramirez. Realizing Cruz has the talent to race, Lightning helps her compete in his place, and she wins. McQueen accepts his new role as a mentor while staying connected to racing.

Turbo

Turbo is about a garden snail named Theo who dreams of becoming a fast racer. After a freak accident gives him incredible speed, he enters the Indy 500 with the help of new friends. Despite being a tiny underdog, Turbo races against human drivers and proves that no dream is too big and no dreamer is too small.
